Sweet Embellishments

We are the alternative to buttercream frosting!

We bake fresh every morning!

Each creation is an individual serving of an embellished dessert that, after one bite, will make you crave for more. They are not overly rich and heavy on the sugar. They are the perfect serving size packaged in pastry boxes for easy carrying and sharing - all for the perfect price. Our goal is to share these treasures and allow your taste buds to experience fun and tasty creations for all of your special occasions or no occasion at all.
We are here to help celebrate "Happy Birthdays" as well as "Happy Thursdays."

Please, We Highly Recommend to Pre-order 4-7 Days In Advance. Larger orders for Big Parties, Events and Weddings must be order ASAP OR at least 8-12 Days In advance. (Late Pre-orders will be subject to what we are able to make with the time that was provided).
